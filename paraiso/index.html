Who We Are?

Paraiso Furniture is a family-owned furniture business located in Paraiso, Cartago, Costa Rica. With a legacy of 10 years of dedicated service, we take immense pride in providing high-quality and exquisite furniture solutions for kitchens, bedrooms, and offices. Our commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction sets us apart as a trusted name in the furniture industry.

What We Do?

At Paraiso Furniture, we specialize in creating beautifully crafted furniture pieces that elevate the aesthetics and functionality of your living and working spaces. Our skilled artisans combine traditional techniques with modern designs to produce a wide range of furniture products tailored to meet your specific needs and preferences. Our Products: Kitchen Furniture: We offer a diverse selection of kitchen furniture, including custom cabinets, kitchen islands, dining sets, and storage solutions. Our kitchen furniture combines elegance and functionality, helping you create a warm and inviting space for cooking and gathering. Bedroom Furniture: Transform your bedroom into a serene oasis with our exquisite bedroom furniture collection. From stylish bed frames and comfortable mattresses to spacious wardrobes and nightstands, we have everything you need to achieve a restful and beautiful bedroom. Office Furniture: Enhance productivity and comfort in your workspace with our thoughtfully designed office furniture. Whether you need ergonomic chairs, functional desks, or storage units, we have the right solutions to make your office a conducive environment for work.

Where Are We?

Our showroom and workshop are conveniently located in the picturesque town of Paraiso, Cartago, Costa Rica. Here, we welcome you to explore our vast selection of furniture designs and speak with our friendly staff who are always ready to assist you in finding the perfect pieces for your home or office. At Paraiso Furniture, we are passionate about creating lasting furniture pieces that reflect our dedication to quality and craftsmanship. Thank you for being a part of our journey over the past decade, and we look forward to continuing to serve the community with the finest furniture solutions for many years to come.
